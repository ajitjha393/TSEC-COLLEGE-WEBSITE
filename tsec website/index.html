About Us

Established in 1983, Thadomal Shahani Engineering College is founded by the Hyderabad (Sind) National Collegiate Board. Thadomal Shahani Engineering College (TSEC) is recognized by the Government of Maharashtra. This college is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), Affiliated to the University of Mumbai, TSEC is graded as an ‘A’ grade educational institution. It offers a 4-year Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) Degree program in Information Technology, Computer Engineering, Electronics & Telecommunication , Biomedical Engineering, Biotechnology and Chemical Engineering.
